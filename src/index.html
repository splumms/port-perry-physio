Physiotherapy Associates of Port Perry

Move Better, Live Better

WHO WE ARE

Our goal is to provide evidence-based treatment for your optimal recovery.

We are a team of health care professionals in Port Perry that works together to bring you quality physiotherapy care for your injuries, sports injuries, impairments and rehabilitation. We work collaboratively with you and your medical team to ensure optimal recovery.

To ensure quality care, our therapists are fully supported in the pursuit of advancing their education. This keeps their knowledge and skill base up to date with the most recent and evidence based forms of practice.

Services
WHAT WE DO

We provide physiotherapy treatment services for a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Muscular Strains and tendinopathies
  • Joint ligament sprains
  • Post-operative rehabilitation
  • Arthritis
  • Neck and Low back pain
  • Sciatica
  • Sports Injuries
  • Repetitive Use Injuries and Repetitive Strains
  • Incontinence/Pelvic floor rehabilitation
  • Concussion assessment and treatment
  • Vertigo
  • Motor Vehicle Injuries
  • Neurological Impairments
